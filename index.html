PROTEIN

Whey protein is a high-grade protein obtained from milk while making cheese. When milk curdles, it divides into solid curds and liquid whey. The liquid whey is transformed into whey protein powder, which is frequently utilized in nutritional supplements.


-Whey protein includes all nine necessary amino acids, which makes it a full protein. It is a commonly used supplement by athletes and bosybuilders to enhance muscle growth


-Whey protein is quickly absorbed by the bosy, aiding in post-workout recovery.


-Whey protein can assist in shedding fat by making you feel satisfied and content after eating.

CREATINE

Creatine is a compound that is naturally present in muscle cells. It has a crucial function in generating energy, especially in activities that are short but intense such as weightlifting, sprinting, and explosive movements. Many athletes and fitness enthusiasts frequently use creatine supplements to improve muscle strength, power, and overall performance during exercise.


-Creatine boosts the capacity to produce power while engaging in activities that require strength, such as weightlifting or sprinting. It works well for tasks needing quick bursts of energy, like lifting heavy objects or sudden movements.

PRE-WORKOUT

A pre-workout supplement is a blend created to boost athletic performance when consumed prior to working out. It usually includes a combination of components that enhance energy, stamina, concentration and power, assisting you in optimizing your exercise session.


-Pre-workout supplements with caffeine and other stimulants can give a powerful snergy boost, making you more alert and prepared for tough workouts, particularly when you're fatigued or sluggish.


-Beta-alanine and creatine aid in delaying muscle fatigue by decreasing lactic acide buildup and boosting energy production. This enables you to prolong your exercise sessions and finish additional sets or repetitions without getting tired as fast.
